# Multiscale Suite Blueprint

## Research lanes

1. architecture + premise gating
2. proxy bridge
3. logit lens and head attribution
4. component patching and density/window stress
5. scale synthesis
6. attention-probe diagnostics as a non-causal support lane

## Attention probes

Attention probes are included here as a search-reduction tool, not as claim-bearing causal evidence.

- good use: identify candidate layers and representations worth testing
- bad use: claim that a decodable feature is therefore used causally by the model

## Final conclusion rule

The final three-model story must separate:

- hard architecture facts
- behavioral facts
- proxy facts
- causal intervention results
- interpretive unification

Any unified theory must be explicitly anchored to those layers of evidence.
